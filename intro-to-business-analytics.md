## ðŸ“Œ KPIs vs Metrics (Very Important)

### Metric
A **measurement** of an activity  
Example:
- Total employees
- Monthly revenue

### KPI (Key Performance Indicator)
A **strategic metric** tied to business goals  
Example:
- Profit Margin
- Employee Turnover Rate

> **Executives track KPIs, not raw metrics.**
